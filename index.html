Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to your repair questions. Contact us for more details.

What is the fee for on-site visits?
The charge is FREE per visit (excludes diagnosis and labor). Spare parts are charged separately.
How soon can a technician arrive?
Standard service is same or next day. Emergency slots are available.
Is there a warranty on repairs?
Yes, we provide warranty upto 1 year based on our guidelines for the same issue. Physical damage or new faults are not covered.
Can I get a cost estimate before repair?
Yes, we diagnose the issue first and provide a transparent quote before starting any work.
Do you repair old or outdated devices?
Yes, if parts are available. We will inform you if repair is not cost-effective.
What if my device isn't fixed?
We offer a 100% money-back guarantee on the service fee if we cannot resolve the issue, after examination.
